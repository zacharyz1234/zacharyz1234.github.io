About Me

Background

Hello, my name is Zachary Zeldin! I am a junior computer science student studying at East Carolina University. My goal for the future is to enter the video game industry as a programmer.

Why Game Dev?

I've wanted to work in the video game industry since I was a child. As someone who moved around a lot throughout my childhood, one way of staying connected with friends or simply keeping myself entertained was through video games. I found that video games are one of the best ways to stay connected or make connections, no matter how far you are across the world from others.

It was during the COVID-19 pandemic when I truly experienced just how true this was. Having just moved to a new state only 2 months before the pandemic shut everything down, I didn't have many in-person friends, and on top of that, the shutdown made it impossible to go out and make any. Luckily for me, I was able to stay connected with my childhood friends through video games. We would spend our days jumping into Fortnite games or letting our creative juices flow in our Minecraft world. No matter what we did, being able to stay connected and have fun through video games is one of the few things that kept me from spiraling into a true depression.

My Goal

The connections that video games helped me build and the experiences to be had with others are the whole reason I was inspired to join the video game industry in the first place. My goal is to hopefully take part in the development of a video game that can inspire someone else in the same way I was.
